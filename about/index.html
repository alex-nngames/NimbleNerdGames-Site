Alex Cockcroft

I'm an indie game developer from Leeds, UK, fuelled by creativity and a passion for crafting unforgettable games. Designing and bringing ideas to life on screen is a joy for me, and I'm always striving for perfection in every project. My journey began on my 19th birthday with my first iPod Touch—after hours of playing, I knew I wanted to build my own mobile games.

From concept to final polish, we focus on gameplay first and design with our players in mind. We are constantly experimenting and growing to make better experiences for everyone.
